College Name:
Siddharth Institute of Engineering & Technology (SIETK), Puttur, Andhra Pradesh, India.

Location:
The college is located at Siddharth Nagar, NarayanaVanam Road, Puttur, Chittoor District, Andhra Pradesh 517583, India.

Establishment:
Siddharth Institute of Engineering & Technology (SIETK) was established in the year 2001.

Affiliation and Approval:
The college is affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Anantapur (JNTU-A) and approved by AICTE, UGC, and accredited by NBA and NAAC (A+ grade).

Campus and Infrastructure:
SIETK has a spacious campus spread over approximately 30 acres with modern infrastructure including well-equipped classrooms, laboratories, library, seminar halls, workshop spaces, and sports facilities.

Student Community:
The college supports a vibrant student community with a variety of academic and extracurricular activities.

Admission Process:
Admissions are offered for various courses based on entrance examinations such as AP EAPCET for B.Tech, AP POLYCET for diploma, AP ICET for MBA and MCA, and AP PGECET for M.Tech.

Programmes Offered:
SIETK offers multiple programs at Undergraduate, Postgraduate, and Diploma levels, including:
- Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech)
- Master of Technology (M.Tech)
- Master of Business Administration (MBA)
- Master of Computer Applications (MCA)
- Polytechnic Diploma programs.

Fee Details:
Approximate fees for the B.Tech program are around INR 72,000 per year and approximately INR 3,00,000 for four years. Hostel facilities are available for students.

Library:
The Central Library supports academic and research needs with a large collection of books, journals, e-resources, project reports, and digital learning materials.

Placements:
SIETK has a placement cell that assists students with training and recruitment activities. The college has achieved placements in reputed companies, and the median package is competitive with industry standards.

Faculty:
The college has experienced and supportive faculty members who guide students in academics, research, and overall professional development.
